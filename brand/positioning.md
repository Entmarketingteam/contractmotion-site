## Primary Positioning

Angle: Contract Motion (the literal frame)
Statement: Most outbound is motion without contracts. Most contract data is contracts without motion. ContractMotion is both — signal intelligence on contracts forming + outbound that puts you on the shortlist before the RFP exists.
Psychology: Names the exact gap in the market. The brand name becomes the thesis. Owners chasing contracts immediately recognize the frame.
Headline direction: "The contract has a name on it. It should be yours."
Best for: Any cluster where the buyer defines themselves by the contract they're chasing — works horizontally across builders, commercial trades, doc destruction, govcon, EPC.

Sophistication: Stage 3 — mechanism needed
Primary alternative: Buy a database subscription (ConstructConnect, GovWin, BidPrime) and chase RFPs already wired for someone else; or hire a generic lead-gen agency that doesn't know vertical signals.

Saturated claims (avoid):
- "More leads / better leads"
- "AI-powered outbound"
- "We book meetings for you"
- "Find your next project"
- "Win more bids"

White space identified:
- Nobody combines signal intelligence + outbound execution as one product.
- Cross-vertical contract intelligence — every competitor owns one vertical (GovWin = govcon, ConstructConnect = commercial construction); nobody owns "any business chasing contracts."
- The "we don't sell leads, we sell motion on the right deals" frame is unclaimed.
- Mid-market trades + services (doc shred, pharmacy, MEP, builders) have no champion for unified contract-signal outbound.
